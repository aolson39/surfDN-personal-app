About

Surf Syndicate is dedicated to guiding digital nomad surfers to their perfect destination. As digital nomads ourselves, we understand the DN's daily worries and needs. We make sure you know about where to score some sweet waves, what the wifi situation is like and the cost of living for each location.
Our goal is to up your wave count, provide you with the knowledge to be productive, and encourage you to pay if forward by supporting the local community wherever you end up.
We also encourage you to share information with our team to keep our location postings as accurate as possible.
